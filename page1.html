Preparation

Preparing a room for a new look

Whether it is a new room or an old one the first thing you need to do it tidy it up. Getting all the furniture out and maybe even giving the room a new coat of paint or carpet. The room I am using for this example is 4.85m wide and 4m long, knowing these measurements are vital to you achieveing the dream room you desire. Don't forget to touch up your window and door frames as well.

Now that one the room is painted and carpetted you can move onto the Planning stage of this tutorial.
