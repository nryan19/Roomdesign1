Selecting the appropriate furniture and furnishings

Selecting a colour pallete and a theme are important for a good room design, for this room I have decided on rustic-modern dark brown timber furniture, with green fabic finishes such as bed sheets, curtains and house plants.

The best things to look for when buying for a bedroom are bedroom sets that include the bed frame, the dresser and a bed-side table.
This Willow Creek Queen 4 Piece Bedroom Set from Living Spaces costs only $495
Make sure you keep a measurement of the furniture to ensure that they will fit in your new room. this particular set's dimensions are;
Queen bed frame: 1.6m Wide x 2.12m Long x 1.37m High
Bed-side table: 0.6m Wide x 0.4m Long x 0.63m High
Dresser: 1.5m Wide x 0.4m Long x 0.63m High
Mirror: 1m Wide x 0.03m Thick x 0.9m High
 

I have chosen this Walton 60 Inch TV Stand as it fits my size requirements, theme and colour and for only $295 from Living Spaces it fits the set budget.
Tv Cabnet: 1.5m Wide x 0.43m Long x 0.68m High

For the TV i have chosen the LG UJ634T UHD 55" 4K HDR Smart LED LCD TV for it's perfomance, convienence and video resolution, at $998 at JB-HI-FI this TV also has the option of being wall-mounted.
